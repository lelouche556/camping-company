Who are we?

Welcome to Camping Co. We are the creators and believers of an alternate way of exploring the world. Camping co. is India's first self-drive over-land expedition venture.

Nature has a subtle way of enhancing consciousness, mindfulness, and awareness. With this belief, Camping Co. was created to provide a platform to have an immersive experience with nature. We enable travelers to explore the unexplored with the help of SUVs, jeeps equipped with rooftop tents and camping gears for a comfortable overlanding expedition. It's a self-drive rental model.

Camping Co. aims to provide a solution for nature tourism and create a community of avid nature-adventure lovers.

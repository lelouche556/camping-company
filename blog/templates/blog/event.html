A 700+KM Journey across Indigenous Land - Meghalaya on wheels is calling! No matter how much you might enjoy staying in a five-star hotel, you will always be stationary, trapped in one place. So, to feed your sense of freedom and adventure, book yourself on an overlanding expedition.

How does it work?

A one of a kind overlanding expedition in Northeast India will take you through the beautiful roads of Meghalaya to explore some breathtaking destinations in overlanding vehicles during the day and you will be waking up in a new and exciting location every morning which will make you connect with Nature like never before.

Who are we?

Nature has a subtle way of enhancing consciousness, mindfulness, and awareness. With this belief, The Camping Company was created to provide a platform to have an immersive experience with nature. It enables travelers to explore the unexplored with the help of SUVs, jeeps equipped with rooftop tents and camping gears for a comfortable overlanding expedition. We are the creators and believers of an alternate way of travel.

Itinerary/Circuit:

We will be driving across the best locations of Meghalaya during our five-nights journey showcasing the best that this part of India, the circuit has been designed to keep your comfort and adventure needs in mind.

Highlights of your expedition:

Day 1 - Welcome! Ever wanted to live off the grid next to a river? Your dream will come true today at our first campsite where you will be surrounded by lush green tea estate on one side and a forest on the other, right next to flowing water of umiam dam. Here you have the entire day to engage in various outdoor activity right from learning kayaking from pro kayakers Ian & David (optional) or go for cliff diving and swimming in a natural pool. Spend your evening by going for a walk in the village or just relish at your campsite while playing with pet rabbits, cats, and dogs. At night let’s gather around, make our bonfire around which we shall enjoy each other company, listen to music and get to know each other better. If you have an artistic side, your audience is waiting so bring along your musical instrument, skit, and a cheering smile.

Day 2 - Next morning, we start our drive to the next destination. Your eyes won’t get enough of this place and your soul will long for the beautiful drive to continue forever. Surrounded by tall waterfalls this island and campsite for the night offers unlimited adventure for a nature lover. Our plan is to trek through meadows, ride a boat, relax on a river beach and do waterfall hopping, you name it and this place has it all. We’ll spend a night tucked away on a hilltop overlooking the river island, later that night we lie down and do some star gazing.

Day 3 - Fan of yoga? We are going to start this day with a session of vinyasa and pranayama to stretch some muscles and focus on breathing so that you are ready for our trek to double-decker root bridge, for the sake of keeping it as the surprise element we will not relieve more about this place but if you really can’t control yourself then just type Nongriat in your google search tab :)

Day 4 - Let’s explore! Nongriat is also famous for it’s hidden waterfalls, crystal blue waters and much more. Get your explorer hat on and bathing suit out cause we will spend a day in paradise. Tonight will be spent relaxing amidst rolling hills at our partner property with all the creature comforts to recharge for your next day!

Day 5 - Remember that crystal clear lake at Dawki that went viral on the internet? We will drive there through the most scenic and panoramic views of Meghalaya amidst the clouds and spot all shades of green during this drive. Choose to do what you like here - there are tons of adventure activities that can be done here right from zip lining across Umngot river to scuba with the fishes. Reach your last campsite of the expedition surrounded by pine trees and share your version of the story of this expedition.

Day 6 - Back to Guwahati. Let’s bid goodbye with a promise to stay in touch!
